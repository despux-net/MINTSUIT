The aesthetic

MINT SUIT builds soundscapes for the edge between memory and dream: warm synths, a future-funk groove, and a layer of lo-fi dust over everything — like a Polaroid of a city you never visited.

It's a suit stitched from neon light and skyscraper windows — R&B, ambient, and synthwave sewn with the same thread: nostalgia, elegance, and a quiet melancholy calm.
